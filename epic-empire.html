Epic Empire

Role: Unity Developer Platform: Mobile (Android/iOS) Genre: Strategy, RPG

Project Overview

Epic Empire is an immersive hotel management simulation game that puts players in the role of a hotel tycoon. Players design, build, and manage their own luxury hotel empire, from small boutique hotels to massive resort complexes. The game features detailed management mechanics, guest satisfaction systems, and strategic business decisions that impact the success of your hotel chain.
